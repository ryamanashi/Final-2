Why should you visit Asakusa?

For centuries, Asakusa has been a center of so-called 'Edo-Culture" where people in the capital has developed a unique culture of Japan before the visit of US in 19th century. Sensouji is probably the most iconic temple in Tokyo area where numbers of tourists visit to wish. Just a few minutes away from Sensouji,Kaminari-Gate is also a notable tourist spot with its infamous Kaminari-Chouchin. If you enjoyed traditional and typical Asakusa enough, have some walk to Tokyo Sky Tree, which is currently the second tallest building in the world. Soramachi is located underneath and you could find literally anything from unique sweets to some Tokyo gifts for your friends.
