Rinkai-Area (Odaiba)

Geographically speaking, Tokyo is facing a beautiful view of Tokyo Bay. Odaiba is such an unique area where all of the lands are made with landfill in bay area. While other areas such as Shibuya and Asakusa have become the symbol of current Tokyo, Odaiba is a symbol for the future of this metropolis. Odaiba shows infinite possibilities even in this such a developed city, where you can find variety of beautiful sights and enjoyable events.
