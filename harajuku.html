Why should you visit Harajuku?

Harajuku is very close to Shibuya district, so you could visit two in one day! While Omotesando-Hills and Laforet are the main shopping plazas in this area, you could find any kind of clothing, accessary brands in Omotesando-St. If you're into Kawaii stuffs and Animes, Takeshita-Stis a place for you to explore, where you could find any items in reasonable prices. Food is a significant feature in this area. Crepe is nothing else compared to other sweets in this area, while other sweets and foods are quite various and tasty. You could find so many cultures in this area where diverse people gather to express their view of the world. If you are looking for underground and unusual side of Tokyo, Harajuku is a whole new world for you.
