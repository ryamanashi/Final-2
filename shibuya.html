Why should you visit Shibuya?

Shibuya has so many experiences to offer to foreign tourists who are not used the craze of modern Tokyo. Shibuya Crossing is one of the biggest and busiest crossing with millions of people use everyday. Right behind the crossing, TSUTAYA has officially the busiest Starbucks in the world where you could view all people moving in the crossing. If you like any genres of music to shop, TowerRecords have diverse music from all over the world. A fan of arcade games? Enjoy classic and new games in Club SEGA Shibuya. Wants to enjoy the nightlife? Have some great time in various nightclubs in Center Arcade! Shibuya is a gate for everything you are looking for.
